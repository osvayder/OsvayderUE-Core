# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be practical, technically rigorous, and welcoming to contributors with different levels of Unreal Engine, MCP, and tooling experience.

Participants are expected to communicate respectfully, assume good faith, and focus critique on the work rather than the person.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear, specific, and constructive in issues, pull requests, reviews, and discussions.
- Respect maintainers' scope decisions, especially around public-readiness, security, and private-data hygiene.
- Provide reproducible evidence when reporting bugs or validation results.
- Sanitize logs, screenshots, and examples before posting them publicly.
- Accept that maintainers may close issues that are out of scope, duplicate, not reproducible, or unsafe to discuss publicly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information, credentials, logs, provider transcripts, or local project details.
- Posting exploit details or sensitive vulnerability information in public issues.
- Repeated off-topic demands, spam, or attempts to bypass maintainer decisions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other moderation actions needed to keep the project safe and focused.

If you need to report conduct concerns, use the repository's available maintainer contact or moderation channel. Do not include secrets or sensitive vulnerability details in public reports.
